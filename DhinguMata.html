Dhingu Mata Temple

Dhingu Mata Temple is a well-known and famous religious place, situated at Sanjauli. You can easily watch various places including Shimla town, the valley of Naldehra, and other distant mountains from this place. Various populated locations are there nearby this place including HB colony, Cemetery road, Dhingu Dhar, Chaulanti, Navbhar, Bhatta Kuffar, and Shanan along with the places, situated on the opposite side of Sanjauli-Dhalli tunnel. Sanjauli is just situated beneath the famous Jakhu Hill. The particular temple is dedicated to the Goddess Durga. .

History

The Dhingu Mata Temple has a rich history that dates back to more than a century ago. It is believed to have been established by ancient sage who recognized the spiritual power of the area. Maa Dhingu Devi is referred to as the goddess who grants wishes. May your visit to Shri Dhingu Mata Temple be filled with profound moments of spiritual awakening and inner peace. A sacred place for the Hindu community. Nestled in the serene surroundings of Sanjauli a suburb of Shimla town, just 7 km from the Ridge Shimla, this temple offers a tranquil atmosphere for devotees to connect with the divine. Located atop Dhingu Hill, at a height of 2,390 meters above sea level, the temple holds great significance among the locals and tourists alike.
